Browsing & Searching Recipes

The main page at /recipes/ shows all recipes in alphabetical order. Use the filter bar at the top to narrow by cuisine, protein, or dish type. Tick Wishlist only to see recipes you want to try. The search filters update the list immediately without reloading the page.

Click any recipe name to open its full detail page, where you can see ingredients, steps, cook history, and linked recipes.

Adding a Recipe — URL Import

Click ⬇ Import on the browse page. Paste the URL of a recipe page and click Fetch & Extract. The app fetches the page and uses Claude AI to extract the recipe details automatically.

Review the extracted data on the review screen — edit any field before saving. Pay attention to the dish type and cuisine dropdowns, which Claude pre-fills but you can override.

A duplicate check runs at review time. If the same recipe name already exists you will be warned before saving.

Adding a Recipe — Document Upload

Click ⬇ Import then choose the Upload a file tab. Upload a PDF or image of a recipe. Claude extracts the structured data the same way as URL import.

If the upload fails with a size error, rasterize the PDF to a compressed JPEG first — NGINX currently limits uploads to around 1 MB.

Adding a Recipe — Manual Entry

Click + Add Recipe on the browse page to open the manual entry form. Fill in as many fields as you have — only the recipe name is required. Ingredients and steps can be added with the + Add ingredient and + Add step buttons.

Editing & Deleting Recipes

Open a recipe's detail page and click Edit to change any field, update ingredients or steps, or adjust the dish type and cuisine.

To delete a recipe, click Delete on the browse page row or on the detail page. Deletion is permanent and removes all associated ingredients, steps, and cook log entries.

Cook Log

Click We made this! on a recipe's detail page or browse row to record a cook. A new log entry is created with today's date. You can add a rating (1–5) and notes afterwards by clicking the entry in the cook history section of the detail page.

If the recipe is on your wishlist, you will be prompted to remove the wishlist flag when you record the first cook.

The cook summary at the top of the detail page shows the total number of times made and the average rating across all cook log entries.

Wishlist

Toggle the Wishlist flag on any recipe's detail page to mark it as something you want to try. Filter the browse page by Wishlist only to see your list.

Wishlist recipes can be used as the selection pool for AI meal planning — choose From wishlist as a planning criterion to draw only from wishlist recipes.

Meal Planning — Manual

Go to Meal Plan to see the weekly grid. Use the dropdowns to assign a recipe to any day and meal slot (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack). Click Save Plan to persist your selections.

Use the ← Prev and Next → arrows to navigate between weeks. Each week is stored separately.

Meal Planning — AI Suggestions

Click ✨ AI Suggest on the meal plan page to open the AI planning form. Choose a scope (single meal, full day, or full week), a composition (mains only or full meals), and one or more selection criteria.

Available criteria: Most beloved (highest rated), Longest since last ate, Most varied cuisine, Prep-ahead friendly, From wishlist, or Surprise me.

Claude generates a list of suggestions drawn only from recipes already in the app. Review the suggestions and accept or reject each one individually. Accepted suggestions are added to the selected meal plan. Rejected suggestions are recorded with an optional reason for future reference.

Optionally select a meal plan from the dropdown before generating — accepted suggestions will be written directly into that plan.

Shopping List

From the meal plan page, click → Shopping List to generate a combined ingredient list for all recipes in the current week's plan. Ingredients are grouped by category and quantities are aggregated across recipes.

The shopping list can be exported as a plain text file using the Export .txt button.

Admin — Managing Picklists

Click ⚙ Admin (top right of every page) to manage the cuisine, dish type, and rejection reason picklists used throughout the app.

Each list shows current values. Use the Add form at the bottom of each section to add a new value. Duplicates are silently ignored. Other is always kept as the last option and cannot be removed.

Rejection reasons entered as free text during AI suggestion review are automatically added to the rejection reasons list for future use.
